  • 灰度变换后灰度直方图的关系

    对于某一灰度值，其直方图数值和灰度变化后对于的灰度值的直方图数值相同

    ∫DBDB+ΔDBHB(DB)dD=∫DADA+ΔDAHA(DA)dD\int_{D_B}^{D_B+\Delta D_B}H_B(D_B)dD = \int_{D_A}^{D_A+\Delta D_A}H_A(D_A)dD ∫DB​DB​+ΔDB​​HB​(DB​)dD=∫DA​DA​+ΔDA​​HA​(DA​)dD

    ∫DBDB+ΔDBHB(DB)dD=∫DADA+ΔDAHA(DA)dD\int_{D_B}^{D_B+\Delta D_B}H_B(D_B)dD = \int_{D_A}^{D_A+\Delta D_A}H_A(D_A)dD ∫DB​DB​+ΔDB​​HB​(DB​)dD=∫DA​DA​+ΔDA​​HA​(DA​)dD

    HB(D)ΔDB=HA(D)ΔDAH_B(D)\Delta D_B=H_A(D)\Delta D_A HB​(D)ΔDB​=HA​(D)ΔDA​

    HB(DB)=HA(DA)ΔDB/ΔDAH_B(D_B)=\frac{H_A(D_A)}{\Delta D_B/\Delta D_A} HB​(DB​)=ΔDB​/ΔDA​HA​(DA​)​

    当ΔDA→0,ΔDB→0\Delta D_A\to0,\Delta D_B\to0ΔDA​→0,ΔDB​→0,

    HB(DB)=HA(DA)dDB/dDAH_B(D_B)=\frac{H_A(D_A)}{dD_B/dD_A} HB​(DB​)=dDB​/dDA​HA​(DA​)​

    代入DB=f(DA)D_B=f(D_A)DB​=f(DA​),

    dDB/dDA=df(DA)/dDAdD_B/dD_A{=}df(D_A)/dD_A dDB​/dDA​=df(DA​)/dDA​

    HB(DB)=HA(DA)f′(DA)H_B(D_B)=\frac{H_A(D_A)}{f^{^{\prime}}(D_A)} HB​(DB​)=f′(DA​)HA​(DA​)​

    因此，灰度变换后图像直方图是变换前直方图与变换函数导数之比。
