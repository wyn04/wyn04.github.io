Maximum Likelihood Estimation

Sample \(\{x^{1},x^{2},\ldots,x^{m}\}\) from \(P_{d a t a}(x)\) ，让模型生成采样的样本 \(\{x^{1},x^{2},\ldots,x^{m}\}\) 的概率最大 \[ \begin{align*} \theta^{*} &=a r g\operatorname*{max}_{\theta}\prod_{i=1}^{m}P_{\theta}\left(x^{i}\right)\ =a r g\operatorname*{max}_{\theta}l o g\prod_{i=1}^{m}P_{\theta}\left(x^{i}\right)\\ &=a r g\,\operatorname*{max}_{\theta}\sum_{i=1}^{m}l o g P_{\theta}(x^{i})\,\approx a r g\,\operatorname*{max}_{\theta}E_{x-P_{d a t a} }[l o g P_{\theta}(x)] \end{align*} \] 减去与 \(\theta\) 无关的第二项 \(\int_{x}P_{d a t a}(x)l o g P_{d a t a}(x)d x\) \[ \begin{align*} &=a r g\operatorname*{max}_{\theta}\int_{x}P_{d a t a}(x)l o g P_{\theta}(x)d x -\int_{x}P_{d a t a}(x)l o g P_{d a t a}(x)d x \\ &=a r g\operatorname*{max}_{\theta}\int_{x}P_{d a t a}(x)l o g{\frac{P_{\theta}(x)}{P_{d a t a}(x)} }d x\ =a r g\operatorname*{min}_{\theta}K L(P_{d a t a}||P_{\theta}) \end{align*} \] Maximum Likelihood = Minimize KL Divergence

Maximum Likelihood Estimation 可以看成让生成图像的分布与真实图像的分布尽可能小

VAE: Compute \(P_{\theta}(x)\)

先sample \(z\) 再得到 \(x\)，\(P(z)\)很容易计算 \[ P_{\theta}(x)=\int_{z}P(z)P_{\theta}\left(x|z\right)d z \]

  • 可以定义\(P_{\theta}(x|z)\)

\[ P_{\theta}(x|z)= \begin{cases} 1,\qquad G(z)=x \\ 0,\qquad G(z)\neq x \end{cases} \]

问题是\(P_{\theta}(x|z)\)可能会几乎都是 0

假设网络的输出\(G(z)\)是Gaussian分布的mean，可得？

G(z)和x的距离越小，\(P_{\theta}(x|z)\)就越大

\[ { {P_{\theta}(x|z)} } \propto\ e x p(-||G(z)-x||_{2}) \]

VAE: Lower bound of \(logP_{\theta}(x)\)

下面的公式中的\(q(z|x)\) 可以是任意的分布，在VAE中是Encoder $$ \[\begin{align} l o g P_{\theta}(x)&=\int_{z}\,q(z|x)l o g P(x)d z \\ &=\int_{z}q(z|x)l o g\left({\frac{P(z,x)}{P(z|x)} }\right)d z\ =\int_{z}\,q(z|x)l o g\left({\frac{P(z,x)}{q(z|x)} }{\frac{q(z|x)}{P(z|x)} }\right)d z \\ & =\int_{z}q(z|x)l o g\left({\frac{P(z,x)}{q(z|x)} }\right)d z+\int_{z}q(z|x)l o g\left({\frac{q(z|x)}{P(z|x)} }\right)d z \end{align}\] \[ 第二项是$K L\left(q(z|x)||P(z|x)\right) $，始终大于0，因此 \] _{z},q(z|x)l o g,(),d z =[l o g,()] $$ 可以通过最大化Lower bound来使\(logP(x)_{\theta}\)最大

DDPM: Compute \(P_{\theta}(x)\)

\[ P_{\theta}(x_{0})=\int_{x_{1}:x_{T} }P\left(x_{T}\right)P_{\theta}(x_{T-1}|x_{T})\ldots P_{\theta}(x_{t-1}|x_{t})\ldots P_{\theta}(x_{0}|x_{1})d x_{1}:x_{T} \] 表示对所有可能的\(x_{1}:x_{T}\)求积分

通过与VAE相同的推导，可以计算得到DDPM \(logP_{\theta}(x)\) 的lower bound

\[ q(x_{1};x_{T}|x_{0})=q(x_{1}|x_{0})q(x_{2}|x_{1})\ldots q(x_{T}|x_{T-1}) \]

计算 \(q(x_t|x_0)\)

其中

推理可得 \[ x_t = {\sqrt{1-\beta_{1} }}\ldots{\sqrt{1-\beta_{t} }} \times x_0+ \sqrt{1-(1-\beta_{1})\dots(1-\beta_{t})} \times \epsilon \] 令\(\sqrt{\overline{\alpha}_{t} } = (1-\beta_{1})\dots(1-\beta_{t})\)， \[ x_t = \sqrt{\overline{\alpha}_{t} } \times x_0+ \sqrt{1 - \overline{\alpha}_{t} } \times \epsilon \]

DDPM: Lower bound of \(logP_{\theta}(x)\)

经过数学推理。。。

https://arxiv.org/pdf/2208.11970.pdf

得到\(logP_{\theta}(x)\)的lower bound： \[ \operatorname{E}_{q}(x_{1}|x_{0})\left[l o g P(x_{0}|x_{1})\right]\,-K L{\bigl(}q(x_{T}|x_{0}){\bigr)}|| P(x_{T}){\bigr)}-\sum_{t=2}^{T}\operatorname{E}_{q}(x_{t}|x_{0})\left[K L{\bigl(}q(x_{t-1}|x_{t},x_{0})||P(x_{t-1}|x_{t})\bigr)\right] \] 第二项\(K L{\bigl(}q(x_{T}|x_{0}){\bigr)}|| P(x_{T}){\bigr)}\)与网络没有关系，不需要考虑

先考虑第三项\(\sum_{t=2}^{T}\operatorname{E}_{q}(x_{t}|x_{0})\left[K L{\bigl(}q(x_{t-1}|x_{t},x_{0})||P(x_{t-1}|x_{t})\bigr)\right]\)

\[ \begin{align} q(x_{t-1}|x_{t},x_{0})&={\frac{q(x_{t-1},x_{t},x_{0})}{q(x_{t},x_{0})} }\\ &={\frac{q(x_{t}|x_{t-1})q(x_{t-1}|x_{0})q(x_{0})}{q(x_{t}|x_{0})q(x_{0})} } \\ &={\frac{q(x_{t}|x_{t-1})q(x_{t-1}|x_{0})}{q(x_{t}|x_{0})} } \end{align} \] 再经过数学推理。。。

得到\(q(x_{t-1}|x_{t},x_{0})\)依旧是Gaussian分布，均值和方差分别是

所以，第三项就是最小化两个Gaussian分布的KL散度

计算两个Gaussian分布的KL散度的公式 \[ D_{\mathrm{KL} }({\cal N}(x;\mu_{x},\Sigma_{x})\parallel{\cal N}(y;\mu_{y},\Sigma_{y}))=\frac{1}{2}\left[\log\frac{\left|\Sigma_{y}\right|}{\left|\Sigma_{x}\right|}-d+\mathrm{tr}(\Sigma_{y}^{-1}\Sigma_{x})+(\mu_{y}-\mu_{x})^{T}\Sigma_{y}^{-1}(\mu_{y}-\mu_{x})\right] \]

  • 更简单的方法

\(q(x_{t-1}|x_{t},x_{0})\)的均值和方差是固定的，与网络无关

对于\(P(x_{t-1}|x_{t})\bigr)\)，只考虑均值

要最小化两个Gaussian的分布的KL散度，就要让他们的均值更接近

\[ x_{t}=\sqrt{\bar{\alpha}_{t} }x_{0}+\sqrt{1-\bar{\alpha}_{t} }\varepsilon \]

\[ x_{t}-\sqrt{1-\bar{\alpha}_{t} }\varepsilon=\sqrt{\bar{\alpha}_{t} }x_{0} \]

\[ {\frac{x_{t}-{\sqrt{1-{\bar{\alpha} }_{t} }\varepsilon} }{ {\sqrt{ {\bar{\alpha} }_{t} }} }}=x_{0} \]

将\(x_0\)代入均值的公式中，再经过数学推理。。。 \[ \frac{\sqrt{\alpha_{t-1} }\beta_{t}\left(\frac{\alpha_{t}-\sqrt{1-\overline{\alpha_{t} }}\varepsilon}{\sqrt{\overline{\alpha_{t} }} }\right)+\sqrt{\alpha_{t} }(1-\overline{\alpha}_{t-1})x_{t} }{1-\overline{\alpha}_{t} } \\ =\frac{1}{\sqrt{\alpha_{t} }}\left(x_{t}-\frac{1-\alpha_{t} }{\sqrt{1-\overline{\alpha}_{t} }}\,\varepsilon\right) \]

上式中只有\(\epsilon\)是未知的，需要网络预测（\(\epsilon\)是加上\(x_0\)生成\(x_t\)的噪声）
